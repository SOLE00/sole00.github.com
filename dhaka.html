Dhaka


Dhaka is the capital city of Bangladesh, in southern Asia. Set beside the Buriganga River, it’s at the center of national government, trade and culture. The 17th-century old city was the Mughal capital of Bengal, and many palaces and mosques remain. American architect Louis Khan’s National Parliament House complex typifies the huge, fast-growing modern metropolis


Location:
Dhaka city is located just north of the Buriganga River, a channel of the Dhaleswari River, in the south-central part of the country. The distance between Dhaka and Chittagong is almost 251 Km by road and 321 Km by train.

Best season for visiting : The best time to visit Dhaka is between the months of February and May when the temperatures are warm, while remaining dry enough to explore the city.

Get in :
From Chittagong-
  • By road: Both AC and Non-AC buses are available on this road. Ticket price for the Non-AC Bus services are 560-600 taka. and AC bus services are 700-1300 taka. Operators: Ena Transport, Soudia Coach Service, Green Line Paribahan, London Express, S.Alam, Shohagh Paribahan, Saintmartin Hyundai, Desh Travels etc.
  • By Air: Air ticket prices can vary around 2500-8000 taka. Available airlines are Biman Bangladesh Airlines, US-Bangla Airlines and NovoAir.
  • By Train: Both AC and Non-AC services are available. The ticket price starts from 285 Tk to highest 1179 Tk. Subarna Express(702), Mahanagar Express(722), Mohanagar Provati(704), Turna Express(742), Sonar Bangla Express(788), Chittagong Mail(2), Karnaphuli Express(4), Chattala Express(68) of Bangladesh Railway is available on this route.
