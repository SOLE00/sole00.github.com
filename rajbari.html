Natore Rajbari

Natore Rajbari(also known as Pagla Raja's Palace, Natore Palace) was a prominent royal palace in Natore, Bangladesh. It was the residence and seat of the Rajshahi Raj family of zamindars. It was the zamindari headquarters of the natore raj. The famous queen Rani Bhabani lived here and after the death of her husband, expanded both the estate and the palace. Like most of the feudal palaces in Bangladesh the palace ruins of Natore are approached through a long avenue, fringed on either side with carefully planted rows of bottle palms.


Location :
The place situated in Natore, a district town about 48 km east of Rajshahi.

Directions :
From Rajshahi- At first you have to go Natore from Rajshahi. You can take a bus which will take 1 hour and 20 minutes to reach Natore. On the contrary local trains are also available. Rickshaws or auto rickshaws can be added directly from anywhere in the city of Natore. Rickshaw fare to Rajbari from Madrasa junction of the city is 20 taka. It is better not to reserve rickshaws or auto rickshaws for travel. Because all the places in the city of Natore have enough local vehicles
