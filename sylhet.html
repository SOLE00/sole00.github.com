Sylhet


Sylhet, the major and beautiful city in north-eastern Bangladesh is located on the bank of the river Surma. With the vast historical and cultural background, Sylhet is one of the oldest cities of Bangladesh. And because of it, this city encounters a number of tourists visiting every year. It is one of the most beautiful and largest cities in Bangladesh surrounded by the Jaintia, Khasi and Tripura hills. The city is well known for its tropical forests and tea gardens.


Location:
The city of Sylhet is situated in the north-east of Bangladesh. Distance from Chittagonng to Sylhet is 378 Km and the distance from Dhaka is 241 Km.

Best season for visiting : January-March is the best time to go to Sylhet due to the excellent all-round climate conditions.
Get in :
From Chittagong-
  • By road: Both AC and Non-AC buses are available on this road. Ticket price for the Non-AC Bus services are 700 Tk. and AC bus services are 1100 Tk.
  • Operators: Ena Transport, Soudia Coach Service, Green Line Paribahan, London Express, S.Alam, BRTC.
  • By Air: Air ticket prices can vary around 7000-10000taka. Available airlines are Biman Bangladesh Airlines, US-Bangla Airlines and NovoAir.
  • By Train: Both AC and Non-AC services are available. The ticket price starts from 315 Tk to highest 1288 Tk. Paharika Express(719) and Udayan Express(723) of Bangladesh Railway is available on this route.
