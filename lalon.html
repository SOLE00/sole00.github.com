Fakir Lalon Shah's Mazaar

Fakir Lalon Shah's Mazaar is the cemetery of Fakir Lalon Shah who was a humanist, mystic, song writter, social reformer and thinker, founder of Baul music (The King of Bauls) as well as he was an icon of religious tolerance. He has composed about 2000 to 10000 songs but 800 songs are authentic.


Location :
There tomb of Lalon Fakir located in Chheuria of Kushtia. It is located in the western part of Bangladesh.Kushtia is one of the districts of Khulna division.

Directions :
From Chittagong-There are so many bus services are available Chittagong To Kushtia Bus Fare. These buses are well equipped with both AC / Non Ac.Once you are in Kushtia then it is very easy to go. Just take a rickshaw from the Kushtia town and ask them to take you to the Lalon Akhra in Chheuria. It is a landmark place in Kushtia. Everyone knows about this place. It should take around 20-30 minutes by rickshaw through the calm town of Kushtia.
