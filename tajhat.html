Tajhat Palace

Tajhat Palace:In the late 18th century, gem trader Mannalal came to Mahiganj for business reasons and later founded Tajhat zamindari. After zamindar Mannalal died, his adopted son Gopal Lal Roy started managing bahadur zamindari.At the beginning of the 20th century, the present Tajhat zamindarâ€™s house was completed with the relentless hard work of about 2000 masons. The zamindar house, which was completed in 1917, cost around Rs 1.5 crore at that time.In 1995, the Bangladesh Archaeology Department registered the Tajhat Zamindar Bari as a protected structure and in 2005 shifted the Rangpur Museum to the second floor of the Tajhat Zamindar House. Location :
Tajhat Palace is situated three km south-east of the city of Rangpur, on the outskirts of town

Directions :
From Rangpur- Jump on an auto-rickshaw to go to Tajhat Palace. It is located just 3 km outside the city.

Visiting Schedule :
Rangpur Museum and Tajhat Zamindar House are open from 10 am to 6 pm during summer, i.e., April to September. And wintertime, i.e., October to March, is open for inspection from 9 am to 5 pm. Rangpur Museum closes from 1 pm until 1.30 pm for lunch.
