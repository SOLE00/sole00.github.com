National Zoo

Bangladesh National Zoo, located in the Mirpur section of the capital city is home to more than 2,000 animals from 165 species and is a popular place to spend the day, both for Bangladeshis and international visitors. Established in 1974, the Bangladesh National Zoo has been developed and improved over the years, and is widely considered to be one of the best zoos in Bangladesh. Previously known as Dhaka Zoo, the name of zoo has been changed to Bangladesh National Zoo in 2015. Main objectives of this zoo are wildlife conservation through collection and breeding of rare and endangered species of wild animals, research & education and recreation.


Location :
Located in Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Main Points to Visit :
The Garden Unit, Veterinary Unit, Fish Unit, Zoo unit, Kids Zone, The library.

Directions :
From Dhaka- There are a lot of options to visit the zoo from any place of Dhaka. But the most easiest and safe is to visit by Metro Prvoati bus service. The bus fare will vary according to the places. It can be around 30-200 taka per seat according to the departing point location.

Suggested Restaurants and Foods:
Restaurants near the zoo in Dhaka City: Summer Fields, Olea, Latest recipe, Prego, Bunka, Star kabab and restaurant.
