Chittagong


Chittagong, officially called Chattogram is the second-largest city in Bangladesh. It is the administrative seat of the eponymous division and district. It hosts the busiest seaport on the Bay of Bengal. The city is located on the banks of the Karnaphuli River between the Chittagong Hill Tracts and the Bay of Bengal. The Greater Chittagong Area had a population of more than 8.7 million in 2017. In 2021, the city proper had a population of more than 5.1 million.


Location:
Chittagong lies about 12 miles (19 km) north of the mouth of the Karnaphuli River, in the southeastern arm of the country.

Best season for visiting : You can visit Chittagong any time. Because,there are many districts and places to visit in Chittagong division. There is no specific season but winter can be better than others to visit and explore Chittagong and Chittagong Hill Tracts.
